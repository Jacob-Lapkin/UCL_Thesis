Utilizing Pose Estimation. It'll blow your mind.

With the help of OpenCV and Mediapipe, the application identifies over 32 joints on the human body throughout the frames of a video. For the application's analysis, the elbows, shoulders and knees are used given their significance in the game of tennis.

Classifying Your Shot With Azure Custom Vision. See for yourself.

To help better pinpoint where each body position is during the tennis stroke, the application utilized Azure Custom Vision to classify the different phases of the stroke.
